Who we are?

Since our beginnings in 1952, Timberland has been dedicated to crafting footwear, clothing, and accessories designed to withstand the most extreme conditions without compromising on style.

Our commitment lies not only in creating quality products but also in protecting the planet and supporting communities. That’s why we’ve implemented sustainable practices at every step of our process
